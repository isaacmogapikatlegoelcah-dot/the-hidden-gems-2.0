About Me

Hello, I'm The Hidden Gems BW

Welcome to my personal blog and platform dedicated to showcasing Botswana's hidden treasures! I'm a passionate advocate for cultural preservation and sustainable tourism, with a deep love for my country's rich heritage.

My journey began while studying Computer Science, where I discovered the power of technology to share stories and connect people across distances. I realized that many of Botswana's most beautiful places and cultural traditions remained unknown to the wider world.

This platform is my contribution to changing that narrative. Here, I combine my technical skills with my passion for exploration to document and share the wonders of Botswana while also showcasing my professional journey.

When I'm not coding or researching, you can find me exploring remote areas of Botswana, meeting with local communities to learn about their traditions, or photographing the stunning landscapes that make this country unique.

My mission is to inspire appreciation for Botswana's rich identity while developing skills that can contribute to sustainable development and cultural preservation.
